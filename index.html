Hi, I’m Free Hoebeke

I’m 27 years old and I've been living in Brussels for years. I met my girlfriend here and we are currently living in Jette, together with our 2 cats.

The first time I had to think about a possible career as part of a school task, I already thought about coding.
Unfortunately, at the age of 12 I still listened to my parents. So that same evening, my dad told me it wasn’t a good idea because coding could be too easily done abroad by cheaper workforce.
From that moment on, I never thought about coding as a career possibility. Instead, for years, I followed some tutorials and started coding on my own on a very basic level, just for fun.

Tired of only trying trough tutorials and thanks to a boring job, I finally took a big step forward and started coding lessons in 2019 at Erasmus Hogeschool Brussel to give myself a last chance to prove that I could do something serious with coding. A Facebook ad and a pandemic later, and here I am, (soon) living my younger self’s dream!
